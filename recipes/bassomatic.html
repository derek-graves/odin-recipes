Terrific Bass

Descrtiption

First introduced on Saturday Night Live in 1976, 'Terrific Bass' is a delicious and smooth drink made from a whole bass.

If you love fish, smoothies, or both, you're sure to love this American classic.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 whole bass

Steps

  1. Place all of the water into the Super Bass-O-Matic '76.
  2. Place the bass--yes, the whole bass--into the Super Bass-O-Matic '76.
  3. Run the Super Bass-O-Matic '76 until the mixture reaches your desired consistency. The smoother, the better.
  4. Enjoy!
